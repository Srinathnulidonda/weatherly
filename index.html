About Nalanda High School Armoor

Founded in 2004, Nalanda High School Armoor is a beacon of educational excellence, offering the best school experience in Telangana.

Nalanda High School in Armoor is dedicated to providing holistic education with top faculty and modern facilities in Mamidipally, Telangana. Our curriculum blends traditional values with modern teaching methods to prepare students for future challenges.
